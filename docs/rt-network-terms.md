# RT Network — Founding Member Agreement (template)

*This is a plain-language starting point, not a substitute for legal review before real contracts are signed. Have a lawyer confirm this against German civil code (BGB) commission/Vermittlung rules before relying on it commercially.*

## Parties

Rising Tide Collective ("RT Network", "we") and **[FILL: Provider legal name]** ("Provider"), a venue / catering / DJ & sound / crew / photography provider based in Berlin ("the Service").

## 1. What RT Network does

RT Network operates a booking platform where clients assemble event packages and submit them for confirmation by the providers they select. RT Network introduces confirmed, pre-checked booking requests to the Provider via a private "backstage" link. RT Network does not employ the Provider and is not a party to the underlying service contract between Provider and client unless separately agreed.

## 2. Commission

- **Founding rate: 2%** of the gross amount of each **confirmed and completed** booking, locked for **24 months** from the Provider's first confirmed booking through RT Network.
- After the 24-month founding window, or for providers who join after the founding period closes, the **standard rate is 10%**.
- No commission applies to declined, cancelled, or never-confirmed requests.
- Until payments are processed through the platform (a future phase), the Provider invoices the client directly for the full amount; RT Network invoices the Provider for commission on completed bookings, monthly.

## 3. What's free

Being listed in the RT Network catalog, receiving booking requests, and using the backstage confirmation portal cost the Provider nothing. The commission in Section 2 is the only fee, and it applies only to bookings that go ahead.

## 4. Provider responsibilities

- Confirm or decline requests promptly (target: within 48 hours) via the backstage link.
- Honor the price and terms shown at the time of confirmation.
- Maintain any licenses, insurance, or permits required to legally provide the Service in Berlin.

## 5. Term & termination

Either party may end this arrangement at any time with written notice (email to hello@risingtide.store is sufficient). Confirmed bookings already in progress remain subject to these terms through completion.

## 6. Data

Provider contact details and booking history are processed per RT Network's [Datenschutzerklärung](#datenschutz). Backstage access is via a private capability link; the Provider is responsible for not sharing it.
